Issue Observed During Teleoperation
==================================

- The gripper can touch and sometimes briefly grasp the microwave/cabinet handle.
- While manually pulling in teleop, the grasp is not stable and the gripper slips off.
- Result: the user cannot reliably continue opening the microwave/cabinet by teleop-only pulling.

Why Helper Functions Still Work
===============================

- `open_microwave` and `open_cabinet` do not rely on pure manual contact pulling.
- They explicitly drive the object joint motion and continuously track handle position with the end-effector.
- This assisted tracking makes the opening motion much more reliable than raw teleop pulling.

Current Constraint for Strategy
===============================

- Teleop is good for approach, orientation, and initial grasp.
- For the actual pull/open phase, helper-assisted execution is currently more reliable than manual teleop.
